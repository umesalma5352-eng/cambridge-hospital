Cambridge Hospital

Since 1928 | Founded by Dr. Elizabeth Cambridge

With a legacy spanning over 90 years, Cambridge Hospital has been a beacon of healthcare excellence in the community.
Founded by the visionary Dr. Elizabeth Cambridge, our institution has grown into a premier medical center,
renowned for its cutting-edge treatments, compassionate care,
and groundbreaking research.

About Us

OUR MISSION

To improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve through excellence in patient care, education, and research. We strive to be the healthcare provider of choice for our patients.

OUR VISION

To be a leader in clinical excellence and a pioneer in medical innovation, ensuring that every patient receives compassionate care tailored to their unique needs.
